# 🦛 MathHilda: Math is Fun!

Welcome to **MathHilda**, the colorful and engaging math learning app designed for kids! Join Hilda the Hippo and her safari friends on an exciting journey through the world of numbers, shapes, and everyday logic.

Whether you're just starting to count or you're ready to become a master of mental arithmetic, MathHilda offers a playful environment to sharpen your skills and build confidence.

---

## 🌟 Meet Hilda & Friends

Learning is better together! Meet our cheerful safari guides who will cheer you on after every game:

*   **🦛 Hilda the Hippo**: The heart of the safari! She loves big numbers and even bigger splashes.
*   **🦁 Leo the Lion**: Courageous and sharp! Leo helps you stay focused on those tricky multiplication tables.
*   **🦓 Zora the Zebra**: Zora loves patterns! She helps you see the logic in sequences and geometry.
*   **🐻 Bruno the Bear**: Strong and steady! Bruno is your go-to friend for weights, measures, and shopping.

---

## 🎮 The Games

MathHilda features 8 diverse game modes to cover all essential early math concepts:

| Game | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **🧮 Arithmetic** | Master Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, and Division! |
| **⏰ Clock** | Learn to tell time, calculate durations, and manage schedules. |
| **🛒 Shopping** | Practice handling money and calculating change in realistic scenarios. |
| **🧁 Recipe** | Scale ingredients for different numbers of people. Great for logic! |
| **📅 Date** | Understand the calendar and calculate days between events. |
| **📐 Geometry** | Discover shapes, their properties, and calculate perimeters. |
| **🧩 Pattern** | Train your brain by completing arithmetic and geometric sequences. |
| **⚖️ Sorting** | Compare sizes and convert units like meters to centimeters. |

---

## 🔥 Key Features

*   **Adaptive Difficulty**: Choose between **EASY**, **MEDIUM**, and **HARD** to match your current skill level.
*   **Challenge Mode**: Can you handle the heat? Take on 3 random games in a row and try to set a new high score!
*   **Progress Tracking**: View your history to see how much you've improved over time.
*   **Instant Feedback**: Our animal friends provide encouraging messages and track your records.
*   **Kid-Friendly UI**: Designed with large buttons and a clean, colorful layout powered by Jetpack Compose.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack

MathHilda is built with modern Android development practices:
- **Language**: Kotlin
- **UI Toolkit**: Jetpack Compose
- **Database**: Room (for history and high scores)
- **Architecture**: MVVM (Model-View-ViewModel)
